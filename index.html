Schedule

Events, stalls and refreshments will run throughout the day. We'll have a hog roast, licensed bar, ice creams and plenty of other refreshments.

Main arena

Each class is £2 to enter and you can register on the day to enter as many as you like for a chance to win rosettes and prizes.

A full list of the available classes and their times will be announced soon.

Entertainment

Music from BSV Jazz Band and Oaklands College student Bethany Hedges. Treasure hunt, children's craft tent, all-age gladiator duel and lots more entertainment to be announced.

Competitions and Prizes

Thanks to our wonderful supporters and sponsors we have lots of competitions and prizes to be won.

Tweet about the day with the hashtag #StAlbansDogsDay to qualify for our extra special mystery prize draw.
